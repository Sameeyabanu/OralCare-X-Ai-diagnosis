Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

Is my data secure?

Yes, absolutely. All uploaded images and personal data are encrypted using industry-standard protocols. We never share your information with third parties.

How accurate is the AI diagnosis?

Our CNN model achieves 98% accuracy in detecting common dental conditions. However, we always recommend confirming results with a qualified dental professional.

Can I use photos instead of X-rays?

Yes! While X-rays provide the most detailed analysis, our AI can also analyze clear photos of your teeth to detect visible issues like cavities or gum problems.

We offer a free basic tier with limited scans per month. Premium plans are available for unlimited access and detailed reports.
