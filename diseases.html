Dental Diseases Guide

Understanding common dental conditions, their symptoms, and treatments

Dental Cavities (Caries)

Cavities are permanently damaged areas in the hard surface of your teeth that develop into tiny holes.

Common Symptoms:

  • Toothache or spontaneous pain
  • Sensitivity to hot, cold, or sweet
  • Visible holes in teeth
  • Brown or black staining on tooth surface
Very Common

Gingivitis (Gum Disease)

A common and mild form of gum disease that causes irritation, redness, and swelling of your gingiva.

Common Symptoms:

  • Swollen or puffy gums
  • Bleeding when brushing/flossing
  • Bad breath (halitosis)
  • Red or dark red gums
Common

Periodontitis

A serious gum infection that damages the soft tissue and destroys the bone supporting your teeth.

Common Symptoms:

  • Receding gums
  • Loose teeth or new spaces
  • Painful chewing
  • Pus between teeth and gums
Serious

Tooth Abscess

A pocket of pus caused by bacterial infection, occurring at the root of a tooth or in the gums.

Common Symptoms:

  • Severe, throbbing toothache
  • Sensitivity to pressure
  • Fever and facial swelling
  • Foul taste from pus drainage
Urgent

Tooth Sensitivity

Discomfort or pain in teeth when encountering certain substances and temperatures.

Common Symptoms:

  • Sharp pain with cold/hot foods
  • Discomfort with sweet items
  • Pain when breathing cold air
  • Sensitivity to acidic foods
Very Common

Oral Thrush (Candidiasis)

A fungal infection caused by Candida yeast that develops on the mucous membranes of the mouth.

Common Symptoms:

  • White lesions on tongue/cheeks
  • Redness or soreness
  • Cottony feeling in mouth
  • Loss of taste
Moderate

Oral Infection

Oral infection usually causes pain, swelling. More serious infections can lead to fever,swollen neck glands to other areas.

Common Symptoms:

  • Swelling of gums, lips, cheeks
  • Redness or inflammation in the mouth
  • Bleeding gums, especially when brushing
  • White patches on the tongue, inner cheeks, or gums
Urgent

Bruxism (Teeth Grinding)

A condition where you grind, gnash, or clench your teeth unconsciously while awake or asleep.

Common Symptoms:

  • Flattened or chipped teeth
  • Jaw pain or tightness
  • Headaches starting at temples
  • Disrupted sleep
Common

Oral Cancer

Cancer that develops in tissues of the mouth or throat, including lips, tongue, cheeks, or gums.

Common Symptoms:

  • Sore that doesn't heal
  • Lump or thickening in cheek
  • White or red patches
  • Difficulty swallowing/chewing
Serious
